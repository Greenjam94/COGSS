Why do I need an account?

The reason this site uses account is because it allows us to track who added what data. Only the host of a meet can modify the data, this will ensure other gymnasts will not be able to cheat. By creating an account, you provide us with a means to authenticate who is doing what in the application. Your data will not be used or sold to third parties. Account information (name and) email is only used for authentication and logging.

Thank you!

This site is still under developement, please use at your own risk and do not hesitate to contact me if your data has been modified by someone else or you need help using this website.
